# Ogden Valley City - Excluded Tax Areas

This report lists tax areas in Weber County that are excluded from Ogden Valley City because they contain either or both of the following entities:
- POWDER MOUNTAIN WATER AND SEWER IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT
- SUMMIT ROAD OVERLAY DISTRICT

- **Total excluded tax areas:** 12

- **Areas with only POWDER MOUNTAIN WATER AND SEWER IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT:** 5 (090, 227, 374, 490, 491, 542, 546, 548)

## Rationale

These tax areas are excluded from Ogden Valley City because they serve specific developments or infrastructure (Powder Mountain ski resort area and Summit Road corridor) that are not part of the general Ogden Valley city tax base.
